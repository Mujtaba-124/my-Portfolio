# About Me

I am a Python developer at the Heritage Foundation of Pakistan, specializing in data-driven solutions for heritage preservation and research.

## What I Do

- Build scalable Python applications for heritage documentation and analysis.
- Work with large datasets from archives, surveys, and research projects.
- Use libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, SQLAlchemy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and PySpark.
- Transform raw data into structured, meaningful formats for long-term storage and reporting.

## My Approach

I focus on:

- Efficient data ingestion and cleaning pipelines.
- Analytical workflows that uncover insights from cultural heritage data.
- Designing storage solutions that keep data organized, accessible, and secure.
- Automating processes to handle complexity and volume with reliability.

## Why It Matters

By combining Python development with heritage research, I help preserve Pakistan's cultural legacy through careful data management and analysis. My work ensures that valuable information is stored in a structured manner and made useful for future study and decision-making.
